Hail, Columbia! is a United States Flavor Pack, including overhauled and expanded journal entries, interest groups, and characters, as well as tons of new events unique to the USA and North America.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/xuNvuA3.png[/img]
[list]
[*]Overhauled map of [b]Native North America[/b], including many more indigenous nations than vanilla, as well as new state traits. Claims are assigned appropriately to prevent weird colonization. The American west will take much longer to be fully colonized by the United States than in vanilla.
[*]Overhauled [b]Indian Removal[/b] JE, including more involved tribes and more events.
[*]Overhauled [b]Slavery Debate[/b] JE, including the full historical lead-up to the civil war: Missouri Compromise, Wilmot Proviso, Compromise of 1850, Kansas-Nebraska Act, Bleeding Kansas, Scott v Sandford. The civil war can be triggered at different times or be split along different borders based on the results.
[*][b]Gilded Age[/b] JE showing the rise of corruption and Trusts in the United States, followed by the [b]Progressive Era[/b] of reform and new ideologies.
[*]New permanent journal entry for the US tracking the influence of [b]Manifest Destiny[/b]. You can choose to avoid expansion and entanglements, stick to the historical borders, or promote Manifest Destiny to new heights and incorporate the entire continent into your great republic.
[*][b]Mormon[/b] content, including the exodus to Deseret and the Utah War.
[*][b]New flavor events[/b] for the US, such as the Seneca Falls Convention and the Great Chicago Fire, as well as various american natural disasters like wildfires and tornadoes. The mod includes [b]over 100 new events[/b] in total.
[*]Overhauled Ideologies and Traits for American IGs, including [b]new alternate IG Traits[/b] unlocked during a playthrough.
[*]Historical agitators, commanders, and politicians for America, including every historical president from Martin Van Buren to FDR.
[*]Tweaks to the american political system, including a Term Limit for presidents, dynamic party names, and Constitutional precedents.
[/list]
